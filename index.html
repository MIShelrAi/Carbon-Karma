üåè Climate Awareness

Why Nepal needs urgent climate action

üèîÔ∏è

Himalayan Glaciers Melting

Nepal's glaciers are retreating at an alarming rate, threatening water security for millions. The Himalayas have lost 15% of their ice in the last 40 years.

Impact: Water scarcity, increased flood risks
üå´Ô∏è

Kathmandu Air Pollution

Kathmandu Valley ranks among the world's most polluted cities. Vehicle emissions and brick kilns are major contributors to poor air quality.

Impact: 42,000+ premature deaths annually in Nepal
üå≥

Deforestation Crisis

Despite forest conservation efforts, illegal logging and land conversion continue. Forests are crucial carbon sinks that we must protect.

Solution: Community forestry & reforestation
‚õΩ

Fossil Fuel Dependence

Nepal imports 100% of its petroleum products, spending billions annually. This creates both economic burden and environmental damage.

Solution: Electric vehicles & hydropower expansion
üåæ

Agriculture Under Threat

Unpredictable monsoons and extreme weather events threaten food security for Nepal's agricultural communities.

Impact: Reduced crop yields, farmer migration
üí°

Renewable Energy Potential

Nepal has massive hydropower potential (83,000 MW), but only 2% is currently utilized. Clean energy is our path forward!

Opportunity: 100% clean electricity possible
